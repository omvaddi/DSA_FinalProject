The Knapsack Problem

The Knapsack Problem presents an assortment of items as well as a knapsack. Each item has its own weight and value and the knapsack has a weight limit. The goal of the problem is to maximize the value of the items put into the knapsack without going over its weight limit. Press the solve button below to watch both a greedy and genetic algorithm try to solve the problem.
